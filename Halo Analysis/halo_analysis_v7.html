How To Use
1
Load an LMK2000 .h5 file via the button above or drag & drop anywhere.
2
Click Draw Line, then drag across the halo — from dark background through the bright edge and back to dark.
3
The luminance profile plots automatically. The orange shaded zones are the detected halo regions. The purple marker is the peak.
⊛
Kliknij Auto Detect, aby program sam znalazł najlepszy profil halo — skanuje od centrum w 8 kierunkach i wybiera linię z najsilniejszym efektem.
⊙
Scroll to zoom · Drag to pan · Double-click to reset view.
